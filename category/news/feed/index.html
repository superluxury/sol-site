]]> http://sol.superlux.com/sol-screens-in-atlanta/feed/ 0 SOL, a Sci-fi Original Short by Superlux http://sol.superlux.com/sol-48-hour/ http://sol.superlux.com/sol-48-hour/#respond Tue, 12 Mar 2013 19:26:16 +0000 http://sol.superlux.com/sol/?p=1 SOL, a sci-fi short from Superlux, was shot, chopped and dropped for the 2012 Atlanta 48-hour Film Project. In a field of 84 entries, SOL garnered best actor honors for 11-year-old Kj Saifullah at the 2012 Atlanta 48-Hour Festival. Around 2am on Saturday, June 15, Director Mark Falls selected a story by writer Tom Rittenhouse from roughly 30 scenarios the team had generated since genre selection, held at 7:30pm the previous evening. With a final draft on paper at 5:30am, the cast and crew embarked on a 38-hour frenzy of shooting, cutting, scoring and post production. It was amazingly fun.  Check out the SOL trailer here.
